Account Deletion

How to permanently delete your Nexsa account

⚠️ Important Notice

Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data, including messages, contacts, and preferences, will be permanently removed from our servers.

Step-by-Step Guide

1

Login to Nexsa

Open the Nexsa app on your device and log in using your registered email address and password.

2

Navigate to Settings

Once logged in, tap on the "Settings" tab located in the bottom navigation bar or main menu of the app.

3

Find Delete Account Option

Scroll down in the Settings menu until you find the "Delete Account" button. This option is typically located at the bottom of the settings list.

4

Confirm Deletion

Tap the "Delete Account" button. A confirmation modal will appear asking you to confirm your decision. Read the warning carefully and tap "Confirm" to permanently delete your account.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account?

  • All your personal data will be permanently removed from our servers
  • Your messages and chat history will be deleted
  • Your contacts and connections will be removed
  • Your profile information and preferences will be erased
  • You will no longer receive notifications from Nexsa
  • This action cannot be reversed

Need Help?

If you're having trouble deleting your account or have questions about the process, our support team is here to help.
